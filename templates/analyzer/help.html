Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about using FoodAI

FoodAI typically achieves 85-95% accuracy in identifying common food items and calculating nutritional information. The accuracy depends on image quality, lighting conditions, and whether the food is commonly recognized in our database. For best results, take clear, well-lit photos with all food items visible.

Yes! FoodAI allows you to save all your analyses to your profile. You can access your history from your profile page, where you'll find statistics and trends of your eating habits over time. This feature helps you make more informed decisions about your nutrition.

You can set your dietary preferences in two places: in your profile settings for platform-wide preferences, and on the recommendation page for specific meal suggestions. These include options like vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, low-carb, and many others. You can update these preferences at any time.

We take privacy very seriously. Only analyses you explicitly mark as "Public" will be visible to other users. Your personal profile information, private analyses, and dietary preferences are never shared without your permission. You have full control over what others can see.

For additional support, you can contact our team at support@foodai.com. We also have a community forum where you can ask questions and share tips with other users. For urgent issues, use the Help button in the bottom right corner of any page to access live chat support.
